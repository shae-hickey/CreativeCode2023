DISCO HOUSE

Description

In an effort to deeper investigate parameterized design, I designed and coded an interactive disco house generator. Depending on the user's mouse location, the disco house will shape shift its form, allowing the user to explore an array of creative possibilities by adjusting the parameters and observing the resulting variations.

Reflection

During my first instance into coding with variables, a valuable lesson emerged: the significance of embarking on a coding project armed with a meticulously crafted plan. Initially, I grappled with the daunting task of visualizing the initial shape, determining sizing parameters, and incorporating interactive elements seamlessly. However, I discovered that commencing with a rudimentary code structure and progressively embellishing it proved to be the most effective approach. This journey highlighted the importance of a thoughtful and iterative development process.
