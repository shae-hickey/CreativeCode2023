Happy Birthday To You

Description

We were tasked with recreating a pixel art drawing that our entire class contributed to. My chosen image was that of a birthday cake.

Reflection

Through this exercise I came to the realization that writing in code is very mathematical and almost forces the writer to think in terms of numbers and grids.
